# Universal Learning Engine v0.5 — Learning Analytics

v0.5 adds deterministic, session-only Learning Analytics over the completed-round
records produced by v0.4 Adaptive Learning.

## Highlights

- Latest-round, current-topic, and overall retained-session analytics
- Weighted and mean-round accuracy
- Confidence and correctness-confidence summaries
- Topic, difficulty, round, and learning-pattern breakdowns
- Evidence-qualified strength and weakness summaries
- Non-fatal analytics integration preserving every v0.4 result and control

## Compatibility

The v0.4 CBT, adaptive guidance, recovery priority, recommendations, Retry, Home,
session-only data policy, and generated lesson JSON contract remain preserved.

## Exclusions

No Decision Engine expansion, persistence, background scheduler, notification,
Living OS integration, or Expansion feature is included.
